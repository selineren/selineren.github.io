Selected work

Wandova

iOS app · 2026 · Shipping to the App Store

A production travel tracker built during my internship at Orion Innovation, now launching on the App Store and evolving with user feedback. It tracks visited countries and wishlists on an interactive MapKit world map, syncs in real time across devices via Firestore, and works fully offline with SwiftData persistence.

Under the hood it follows MVVM with a repository layer: SwiftUI views drive an observable app state, which reads and writes through a repository protocol backed by SwiftData locally and Firestore in the cloud. A sync service handles bi-directional updates with an offline queue, retry logic, and per-record conflict resolution.
